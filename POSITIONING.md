# Product positioning

## One-liner

AMP is a protocol and runtime for orchestrating networked AI agents with standardized discovery and matching.

## Who should use Community

- Technical teams that want to prototype quickly.
- Companies that want to validate network agent architecture.
- Projects that prefer self-hosted with full control of the base stack.

## Who should use Enterprise

- Teams that need corporate security and formal governance.
- Operations with SLA requirements, support and complete observability.
- Sensitive and/or regulated multi-tenant environments.

## Direct message to the market

- Community delivers the **functional core**.
- Enterprise delivers **operational reliability and corporate governance**.

## Do not promise (to avoid misalignment)

- Do not sell Community as an immediate replacement for an enterprise platform.
- Do not promise compliance without implemented controls and auditing.
- Do not promise unrestricted scale without benchmarking and published SLOs.

## Short narrative for README/site

"AMP Community provides the open protocol core for building, testing, and operating networked agents. AMP Enterprise adds the controls needed for enterprise environments: advanced security, governance, complete observability, SLA, and dedicated support."
